# Set Pieces — Brand Context

## Overview

**Set Pieces** is a soccer-inspired streetwear and luxury clothing brand founded by Aaditya, blending minimalism, football culture, and sustainability. The brand sits at the intersection of sport, fashion, and forward-thinking design.

- **Website:** [setpieces.co](https://setpieces.co)
- **Platform:** Instagram (managed by Aaditya)
- **Brand name origin:** "Set pieces" = dead-ball situations in soccer (free kicks, corners) — intentional, strategic plays. A metaphor for the brand's calculated, craft-driven approach.

---

## Brand Identity

- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, soccer-inspired streetwear with a luxury sensibility
- **Themes:** Soccer culture, vintage sportswear, sustainability, intentional design
- **Target vibe:** Elevated soccer streetwear — wearable off the pitch, inspired by the game
- **Positioning:** Soccer × fashion × luxury streetwear — a niche with strong differentiation potential
- **Long-term:** A brand with AI-driven sustainable manufacturing as a core technical thesis

---

## Founding & Team

- **Partner** — Co-founder; primarily responsible for graphic design.

## Design Approach

- Strong interest in **soccer badge/crest design systems**
- **"SP" logo** adapted into vintage football badge and classic club-inspired emblem styles
- Exploring: track jackets, hybrid garments (denim + jersey), one-of-one / upcycled pieces
- Visual references: vintage club kits (1980s–2000s), Japanese selvage denim, minimalist luxury

---

## Sustainability Vision

Core technical thesis — not just branding:

- **AI-driven pattern cutting:** Use optimization models to lay patterns on fabric minimizing textile waste
- **Goal:** Build a proprietary AI tool/service for sustainable garment manufacturing — potentially licensed to fashion houses
- **Philosophy:** Upcycling and waste reduction through technology

---

## AI × Fashion Intersection

- Computer vision / optimization for fabric waste reduction
- Trend forecasting (e.g., track jackets as a coming soccer fashion trend)
- Long-term: B2B AI service for sustainable fashion manufacturing, with Set Pieces as proof-of-concept
- Sakkad (this tool) is part of this vision — visual inspiration capture → taxonomy → design sketch

---

## Pricing Philosophy

- Premium streetwear margins
- Student discounts without weakening premium feel
- Price premium pieces at minimum 2× production cost
- Special details (embroidery, badges) justify higher pricing tiers

---

## Marketing Context

- Drop-based, event-aware, community-oriented
- Channels: Instagram, Snapchat, in-person campus selling
- Key moments: homecoming, football games, campus foot traffic
- Positioning: soccer-rooted, streetwear-first, premium but accessible

## Sakkad as a Tool for Set Pieces

This app is directly useful for Aaditya as creative director of Set Pieces:

- Upload inspiration images (vintage jerseys, street photography, fabric textures) → get taxonomy + designer references back
- Group captures by aesthetic direction → generate hand-drawn sketches
- Use as a visual R&D tool for each drop cycle
- Taxonomy should be tuned to soccer-fashion vocabulary (jersey culture, kit aesthetics, football casual, sport heritage)
